PRIVATE VENUES

The Oasis is an event management company that provides private venues for various events, including weddings, mehndi ceremonies, and birthday parties. They may offer a range of services to help plan and execute your event, including venue selection, catering, decor, and entertainment.

A private venue for a wedding event can refer to any location that is not open to the general public and is specifically rented or reserved for a private event, such as a wedding. Some examples of private venues for a wedding event include:

Private estates or mansions: These can be a beautiful and intimate setting for a wedding, with plenty of space for both the ceremony and reception.

Private event spaces: Many cities have event spaces that can be rented for private events, such as wedding receptions or ceremonies. These spaces often have a blank canvas that can be decorated to your liking.

Private clubs: Some private clubs, such as country clubs or yacht clubs, may offer rental of their facilities for private events, including weddings.

Private hotels or resorts: Many hotels and resorts have event spaces that can be rented for private events, including weddings. These venues often have a variety of spaces to choose from, including indoor and outdoor options.

When choosing a private venue for your wedding, it’s important to consider the size of your guest list, the style of your wedding, and your budget. It’s also a good idea to visit the venue in person to get a feel for the space and to ensure that it meets your needs for further information please feel free to contact us.
