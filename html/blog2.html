1. HSY


Hassan Sherhyar Yaseen, better known as HSY, redefines bridal couture, blending contemporary trends with cultural
richness. As the largest South Asian bridal label, HSY excels in intricate designs and vibrant colour schemes.
A global fashion icon, he has not only set the standard but elevated the bridal couture industry. Each creation
is a testament to his mastery, harmonizing modern aesthetics and traditional allure. HSY stands as a symbol of
sophistication, influencing the landscape of Pakistani wedding dress designers with unparalleled elegance and style..
